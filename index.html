About Me

🌱

I am a dedicated environmental justice researcher and climate resilience specialist committed to addressing the intersection of environmental issues and social equity. My work focuses on developing inclusive strategies that protect vulnerable communities from climate impacts while promoting sustainable development.

With expertise in community-based participatory research, climate adaptation planning, and environmental policy analysis, I work to ensure that climate solutions benefit everyone, especially those most affected by environmental injustice.

My approach combines rigorous scientific research with grassroots advocacy, believing that meaningful climate action must center the voices and needs of frontline communities.

Research Areas

🌍 Environmental Justice

Investigating the disproportionate environmental burdens faced by marginalized communities and developing equitable policy frameworks that address systemic inequalities in environmental protection and resource distribution.

🌊 Climate Resilience

Designing adaptive strategies that strengthen community capacity to withstand and recover from climate-related disasters, with emphasis on nature-based solutions and community-led resilience planning.

🏘️ Urban Sustainability

Examining sustainable urban development practices that reduce environmental impacts while improving quality of life, including green infrastructure, renewable energy access, and equitable urban planning.

📊 Policy Analysis

Evaluating environmental policies through an equity lens, analyzing their impacts on diverse communities, and recommending evidence-based improvements to advance both environmental and social justice goals.
